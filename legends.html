Each legend has a tactical ability, a passive ability and a ultimate ability. Each falls under a category namely.

  • Assault

    • Legends used to rush in to deal damage

  • Scout

    • Legends used to locate nearby enemies and reveal to your squad

  • Support

    • Legends used to heal or protect your squad


Here's which category legends falls under

As you can see there are some legends that fall under more than one category. For example lets have a look at the legend Ash.

Ash


  • Tactical Ability

    • Ensnare enemies for a few seconds, which falls under assault

  • Passive Abilty

    • Locate enemies using fallen foes, which falls under scout

  • Ultimate Ability

    • Create a one way portal to enter battles, which falls under assault

Ash has two abilities suited for assault and one ability suited for support which makes a versatile legend to play with.
